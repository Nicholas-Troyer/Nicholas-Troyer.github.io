Screen Printing

Screenprinting is a process that using a mesh screen and squegee, ink is applied to a shirt that has had the design on it, so that the design gets made.

Sublimation

Sublimation uses heat and pressure to apply a design to the desired object via sublimation paper.

Embroidery

Embroidery is like sewing ontop of an already made object, you use a needle and thread to create the design ontop of the object.

DTF Printing

DTF (Direct to Film) is a process that prints a design onto fabric and poly blends, using a similar method to sublimation.

Promotional

Do you want to promote your brand, why not get a cool shirt to do so? If so, Ink'd Impressions has you covered.

Fundraisers

Local community fundraisers are great, and we want to help support that with our shirts.
